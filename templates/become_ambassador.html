Ready 2 Dink Ambassador Program: Official Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Ready 2 Dink Ambassador Program! We believe the best way for Ready 2 Dink to grow is through the passion of players like you. This program is designed to reward our loyal community members for helping us expand the network and bring the best pickleball connections to more courts. By participating, you can earn free lifetime access, exclusive swag, and unique status for your advocacy.

2. How It Works

  1. Register to be an Ambassador & Get Your Unique Code: Every Ready 2 Dink user can access a personalized referral link or code directly within the app.
  2. Share with Your Court: Share your unique code with players in your community, at your local courts, and on social media.
  3. New Players Join: When someone clicks your link or enters your code upon sign-up, they are instantly attributed as your referral.
  4. Verification (The Key): A referral is officially verified and counted toward your progress only after the new user successfully creates or joins their first verified game/play session using the Ready 2 Dink app.
  5. Earn Rewards: As you rack up those verified referrals, you will advance through our tiered program and unlock exciting rewards!

4. Ambassador Dashboard & Status

Within the Ready 2 Dink app, you will have access to your personal Ambassador Dashboard. Here you can:

  • View and copy your unique referral link or code.
  • Track your current number of verified referrals.
  • See your progress to the next reward tier.
  • Access promotional images featuring the Ready 2 Dink logo.
