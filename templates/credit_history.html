About Tournament Credits

Tournament credits are issued in the following situations:

  • Medical Emergency: When you can't participate due to a documented medical issue
  • Tournament Cancellation: When a tournament is cancelled by the organizers
  • Technical Issues: When technical problems prevent fair play
  • Admin Discretion: Special circumstances reviewed by administrators

Note: Credits can be used for future tournament entries and never expire. They cannot be converted back to cash.
